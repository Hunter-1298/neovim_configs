The ultimate hack to vim commands


--- TEXT OBJECT COMMANDS ---
dw - delete a word
ce - change word to end
ct<char> - change until word
dt<char> - delete until word
ciw - change inner word
ci( - change in bracket
ci" - change in quotes
va( - select around 

--- LINE BASED MOTIONS ---
hjkl - left, down, up, right
w - begenning of next word
e - move to end of next word
b  move to begenning of previous word
ge - move to end of previous word
$ - move to end of line
0 - move to beg of line
^ - move to first non white space
:<num> - jump to numberd line


:! - execute command outside of vim
o - open line below cursor in insert mode
